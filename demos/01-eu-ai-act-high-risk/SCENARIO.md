# Scenario: Hiring AI failing EU AI Act Annex IV

An HR screening AI is Annex III high-risk but the manifest is missing most required fields.

## Expected findings

- AC-EUAI-TRAINING_DATA_SUMMARY
- AC-EUAI-EVALUATION_METRICS
- AC-EUAI-HUMAN_OVERSIGHT_PLAN
- AC-EUAI-DATA_GOVERNANCE
- AC-EUAI-TRANSPARENCY_DISCLOSURES

## Why this matters

Under the EU AI Act, deploying this without these fields is a conformity-assessment failure with fines up to €35M or 7% of global turnover.
